## Research Package Structure

A good competitive research package includes:
1. **Landscape overview** — market map, key players, segments
2. **Competitor profiles** — one per major competitor
3. **Differentiation analysis** — where do you win, where do you lose?
4. **Battlecards** — quick one-pagers for sales/CS to use in competitive situations

## Cadence

- **Quarterly**: Full landscape refresh
- **As-needed**: Competitor profile when a new player emerges or a competitor ships something significant
- **Monthly**: Quick scan of competitor releases and pricing changes

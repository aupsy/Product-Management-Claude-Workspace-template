# Strategy

Roadmap proposals, investment cases, strategy documents, and long-range planning.

## What Belongs Here

- Annual or quarterly roadmap documents
- Investment cases / business cases for major bets
- "North star" or vision documents
- Partnership or go-to-market strategy
- Competitive strategy and positioning

## Naming Convention

`document-name-YYYYMMDD.md` — always date-stamp strategy docs so you know which is most recent.

## Tips

- Claude reads these when you ask about strategic tradeoffs or ask it to position a feature idea
- Even a rough brain-dump of "our strategy for Q3" is more useful than nothing
- Archive old versions (don't delete) — context about *why* a strategy changed is valuable
